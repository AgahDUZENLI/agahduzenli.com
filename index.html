01 — About

Hello, I'm Agah.

I started my journey in architecture — designing spaces, thinking about how people move through environments, how light and material shape experience. Then I found myself equally drawn to building in code. Now I sit at the intersection of both: designing systems that are as considered as buildings.

I'm currently pursuing computer science with plans toward a PhD, exploring how computational thinking and design can inform each other. When I'm not at a desk, I'm on a tennis court or behind a camera.
